  • label_outlineWhat is NAVMO?

    NAVMO is a national competition for all students practicing Abacus and Vedic Maths across India to showcase their skills irrespective of which organization/schools/franchisee they belong to.

  • label_outlineWho is organizing NAVMO?

    Mind Power Education (MPE) is organizing the first ever NAVMO competition to give all students across India a chance to participate in a premier Abacus and Vedic Maths competition irrespective of which organization/School/Franchisee they belong to.

  • label_outlineWhat are the different categories in NAVMO ??

    NAVMO is divided into 4 different categories. Students can pick one category from ALPHA, BETA and THETA group and can also pick the GAMMA Group.

    ALPHA GROUP (LKG - Class I) – Direct sums on Abacus.
    BETA GROUP (Class II onwards) – Level 1-5 Individual Categories on Abacus.
    THETA GROUP (Class VI – Class XII) – Level 1 & 2 Individual Categories on Vedic Maths.
    GAMMA GROUP Mind Magic MM (Open for All ) – Mental Calculations without Abacus.
    For MM-1 The students who have undergone level 1 and 2 of Abacus can only participate from alpha and beta group.

  • label_outlineWhat are the round in NAVMO?

    The competition is held at three rounds.The first round will be held in major cities (listed in the Exam center field in registration page).If there are more than 20 students registering from one school/center in a city, Mind Power Education will make an effort to come to your city to conduct the test (even if it’s not listed in the page).

    Top students from the first round will qualify for Semifinal to be held in MPE City center.

    Final round, will be held in Pune, Maharashtra

    First round – Before 30th November 2016
    Semifinal – Before 25th December 2016
    Finals – In the month of January 2017

  • label_outlineWho can participate in NAVMO?

    NAVMO is open to All students practicing Abacus and Vedic Maths across India irrespective of which organization/schools/franchisee they belong to. Gamma Group Mental Maths is for open for all students.

  • label_outlineWhat is the fees for entering in NAVMO?

    Students need to pay INR 500/- for choosing one Group (ALPHA, BETA, THETA or GAMA). If students choose more than one group from the list below, the fees is INR 750/- ALPHA + GAMMA or BETA + GAMMA.

  • label_outlineHow many questions will be there in NAVMO?


    ALPHA Group – 70 Questions with Abacus
    Time duration – 10 min for First Round and Semifinal , 7 min for Final Round.

    BETA Group – Each level 70 Questions Time duration - 5 min

    THETA Group – 70 Question Time duration – 20min

    GAMMA Group – 50 Questions Alpha only Time duration - 10 min
    GAMMA MM 1 to MM 5 (Class 2 onwards) Time duration - 5 min

    Paper pattern will be different for different age groups.

  • label_outlineIf students qualify for finals, how should they arrange for travel to Pune?

    Once the student qualifies for the finals, they should book their own tickets to come to Pune. Mind Power Education can assist students/parents in the accommodation and travel in Pune to go to the examination venue. However, the students/parents need to bear their own cost. If you need assistance in anything, please reach out to us via our contact Us Page.

    Student should bring their Abacus Box and pencil for the ALPHA and BETA Group Student should bring Pen and pencil for THETA group For GAMMA group only Pencil is required. No eraser is allowed in the examination hall .
