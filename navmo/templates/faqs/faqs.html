  • label_outlineWhat is NAVMO?

    NAVMO is a national competition for all students practicing Abacus and Vedic Maths across India to showcase their skills irrespective of which organization/schools/franchisee they belong to.

  • label_outlineWho is organizing NAVMO?

    Mind Power Education (MPE) is organizing the first ever NAVMO competition to give all students across India a chance to participate in a premier Abacus and Vedic Maths competition irrespective of which organization/School/Franchisee they belong to.

  • label_outlineWhat are the different categories in NAVMO?

    NAVMO is divided into 4 different categories. Students can pick one category from ALPHA, BETA and THETA group and can also pick the GAMA Group. § ALPHA GROUP (LKG - Class I) – Direct sums on Abacus § BETA GROUP (Class II – Class v) – Level 1-5 Individual Categories on Abacus § THETA GROUP (Class VI – Class XII) – Level 1 & 2 § GAMA GROUP Mind Magic MM (Open for All ) – Mental Calculations All Abacus Levels

  • label_outline What are the rounds in NAVMO?

    The competition is held at three rounds. § The first round will be held in major cities (listed in the Exam center field in registration page). If there are more than 30 students registering from one school/center in a city. Mind Power Education will make an effort to come to your city to conduct the test (even if it’s not listed in the page) § Top students from the first round will qualify to come for Semifinals and Final round, which will be held on 31st Jan, 2016 in B. K. Birla Center for Education in Pune.

  • label_outlineWho can participate in NAVMO?

    NAVMO is open to All students practicing Abacus and Vedic Maths across India irrespective of which organization/schools/franchisee they belong to.

  • label_outlineWhat are the different prizes you can win in NAVMO?

    Mind Power Education (MPE) will be distributing prizes worth 5 lakhs to students. § GROUP CHAMPION / SCHOOL CHAMPION in Every City § STATE CHAMPION 18 Gold Medals § CHAMPION’s CHAMPION - 21 Cash Prizes Up to INR. 2 lakhs § SCHOLARSHIP OF EXELLENCE IN ABACUS AND VEDIC MATHS 100 nos. RS 4000 each § BEST SCHOOL TROPHY - 10 numbers

  • label_outlineWhat is the fees for entering in NAVMO?

    Students need to pay INR 400/- for choosing one Group (ALPHA, BETA, THETA or GAMA). If students choose more than one group from the list below, the fees is INR 600/- § ALPHA + GAMA § BETA + GAMA

  • label_outlineHow many questions will be there in NAVMO?

    § ALPHA Group- 70 Questions with Abacus time duration – 7 min § BETA Group Each level 70 Questions time duration -5 min § THETA Group – 70 question time duration – 20min

  • label_outlineHow many students will qualify across India for Semifinals?

    MPE is expecting around 250-300 students for the Semifinals § ALPHA Group- 50 students § BETA Group o Level 1 – 80 students o Level 2 – 50 students o Level 3 – 30 students o Level 4 – 20 students o Level 5 - 20 students § THETA Group o Level 1 – 50 students o Level 2 – 30 students § GAMA Group – MM -1, MM-2, MM3, MM4 o Total students – 30 students

  • label_outline If students qualify for Semifinals, how should they arrange travel to Pune?

    Once the student qualifies for the Semifinals, they should book their own tickets to come to Pune. Mind Power Education can assist students/parents in the accommodation and travel in Pune to go to the examination venue. However, the students/parents need to bear their own cost. If you need assistance in anything, please reach out to us via our contact Us Page.

  • label_outlineWhat should the student bring to Exam?

    Student should bring their Abacus Box and pencil for the ALPHA and BETA Group Student should bring Pen and pencil for THETA group For GAMA group only Pencil is required. No eraser is allowed in the examination hall.
