About NAVMO:

NAVMO-National Abacus and Vedic Mathematics Olympiad is a national level competetion,that is primarily conducted to give all student practicing Abacus and Vedic Mathematics, across all levels, to compete in National Platform. The goal of the competetion is to give student of all age group to participate in various phases and win the ultimate prize of Champion of Champions



ELIGIBILITY - All Students practicing Abacus and Vedic Maths across different levels at different Institutions and Schools can participate. The Competetion will be held across four groups Alpha, Beta,Theta & Gamma to give an edge to studnets of all age group to participate in the Annual Brain Game "NAVMO".

Examination Pattern:

  1. Alpha/Beta Group student have to calculate on Abacus.
  2. Theta group is only for Vedic Maths.
  3. Alpha/Beta/Theta Group students can also participate in Gamma Group
  4. Gamma group is Mental Arithmetic Calculation without Abacus



Alpha/Beta/Theta 70 questions
Gamma Group 50 questions
Alpha 10 min
Beta 05 min
Theta 20 min
Gamma MM-1 Alpha only 10 min
Gamma MM-1 to MM-5 Beta only 05 min
*The above pattern is applicable for all levels of Exams.
