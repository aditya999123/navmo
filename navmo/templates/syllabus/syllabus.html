About Navmo:

NAVMO-National Abacus and Vedic Mathematics Olympiad is a national level competetion .that is primarily conducted to give all student practicing abacus and vedic mathematics across all level to compete in national platform the goal of the competetion is to give student of all age group to participate in various phases and win the ultimate prize of champion of champions

Examination Pattern:

  1. Alpha/Beta/ Group student have to calculate on Abacus Theta group is only for vedic maths
  2. Alpha/Beta/ Group student can also participate in gamma group
  3. Gamma group is Mental Arithmatic calculation without Abacus

Alpha/Beta/Theta 70 questions
Gamma Group 50 questions
Alpha 10 min
Beta 05 min
Theta 20 min
Gamma mm-1 Alpha only 10 min
Gamma mm-2 to mm-5 Beta only 05 min
Syllabus:

NAVMO: question paper are more group and level specific please refer to thesyllabus provided in the website- www.mpenavmo.com

10 Days Workshop:

Mind Power Education is organization a workshop for freshers for 10 days to be conducted in school and centers to give all student an edge to participate in NAVMO for introductory level for all group to compete nationwide.

The 10 Days Workshop Deliverables :

  1. Covers introductory level of abacus and vedic maths for different group
  2. includes certification of first level Abacus/vedic maths

  4. include practice worksheets
